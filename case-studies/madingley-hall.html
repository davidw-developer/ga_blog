Madingley Hall Case Study.

A classic, functional design given a modern touch- Madingley Hall is a website which is focussed on showcasing its highly impressive premises and encouraging visitors to book a table for dining or organise an event.

The website was built entirely using Flexbox, and is powered by Craft CMS. I was responsible for providing an instructional demo to the client on how to use the Content Management System.

The website was received very well by the client who was and continues to be a pleasure to work with.
